About Acta Prep

Acta Prep provides personalized, private academic support and test prep services for middle school, high school, and college-aged students anywhere in the world from the comfort of their own homes.

Acta Prep was established in 2016 by Andrew Dole. A professional educator for over a decade, Andrew has served students from all over Fairfield and Westchester counties including New Canaan, Darien, Greenwich, Westport, Rowayton, Stamford, Norwalk, and Round Ridge, NY since 2011.

Andrew earned his Bachelor's degree in Economics and Classical Studies from the College of the Holy Cross in 2009 before completing his Master's degree in Classical Studies at Villanova University in 2011. Andrew tutored peers in Calculus, Economics, Latin, and Greek throughout his years at Holy Cross and Villanova.

For five years, Andrew taught Latin at New Canaan Country School where he assumed various other roles including Chair of the World Languages Department. During this time, Andrew cotninued tutoring local students and expanded his services to include standardized test prep for ACT, SAT, SSAT, and ISEE.

Amidst the Coronavirus pandemic, Andrew transitioned all tutoring and test prep sessions to remote meetings in March, 2020. Thanks to tehcnologies like Zoom, Facetime, Google Docs, and Scribble Together, the "distance" in distance learning has been virutally eliminated.

Andrew strives to provide outstanding and effective acdemic and test prep support tailored to each student's individual needs.
